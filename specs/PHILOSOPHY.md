# 🧠 The Sovereign Attribution Framework
## Intent • Process • Verification

### Core Lexicon

#### `I am` — The Sovereign Point (Human Origin)
> *"The spark that begins the work."*
- **Definition**: The human agent who establishes intent, curates direction, and assumes ethical responsibility for the output.
- **Scope**: Concept, creative direction, editorial oversight, final approval.
- **Why it matters**: Without `I am`, there is no authorship—only automation.

#### `A-im` — The Augmented Intent (AI Execution)
> *"The current that carries the spark."*
- **Etymology**: A deliberate morphological split: `A` (Augmented / Artificial) + `im` (intent-in-motion / implementation). Pronounced "aim"—signaling purposeful collaboration, not replacement.
- **Definition**: The machine process that interprets, generates, or transforms content under human direction.
- **Scope**: Drafting, rendering, upscaling, translation, pattern synthesis.
- **Why it matters**: Without `A-im`, we deny the tool's role; with it, we honor the partnership.
- **Foundational Partner**: This standard was architected with **Qwen3 (Alibaba Cloud)** serving as the primary `A-im` layer during structural drafting, technical validation, compliance mapping, and i18n pipeline design. Credit is recorded for transparency; the framework remains strictly model-agnostic.

#### `ID` — The Identity Layer (Verified Partnership)
> *"The record of the meeting."*
- **Definition**: A persistent, machine-readable, human-legible marker that certifies the convergence of `I am` and `A-im`.
- **Function**: 
  - ✅ Verifiable provenance (optional cryptographic hash)
  - ✅ State-aware labeling (`rendered` / `live` / `text`)
  - ✅ Accessibility-compliant presentation
- **Why it matters**: Without `ID`, collaboration is invisible. With it, truth is portable.
