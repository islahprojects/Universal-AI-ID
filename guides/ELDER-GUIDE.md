## What is AI ID?
When you see **🧬** in the corner of a photo, video, or article, it means:
> *"This was made with help from artificial intelligence — and a human is still in charge."*

It's **not a warning**. It's a promise: **You deserve to know how things are made.**

---

## How to Spot It

| Where You See It | What It Looks Like | What It Means |
|------------------|-------------------|---------------|
| 📺 **Video / Movie** | `AI was here 🧬` (bottom-right corner) | This scene used AI to create or enhance visuals |
| 📱 **Live Stream** | `AI Here 🧬` (gently pulsing) | AI is helping right now (like filters or translation) |
| 📄 **Article / Report** | `AI ID 🧬` (near title or footer) | Words were drafted or checked with AI assistance |

## Common Questions

### 🔹 Is this fake?
**No.** 🧬 means "made with care, with help from both human and computer."

### 🔹 Can I trust this?
**Yes.** 🧬 means the creator is being open and honest with you.

### 🔹 What if I don't like AI?
You can still enjoy the work — now you choose with full knowledge. The 🧬 gives you that choice.

### 🔹 Will it block my view?
**No.** It's designed to be gentle and unobtrusive — visible but not distracting.

### 🔹 What if I can't see it well?
The mark automatically adjusts for high-contrast mode and large text. If you use accessibility features on your device, the 🧬 will respect them.
